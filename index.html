FDE Solution

The Field Data Enablement solution currently consists of the FDE Gateway and the Field Data Enabler PN App:
 

  • The FDE Gateway is a connector that also reads previously inaccessible I/O data from the field devices and maps it directly in the IT level or the cloud.

  • With the integration of the Field Data Enabler PN App, data from the field level is mapped directly in the IT level  or the cloud without having to take a detour via the OT level.

Efficient data management
Display data from the field level directly in the IT level or cloud. Without detours via the OT level and programming efforts at the PLC. Without additional installation effort.


Reduction of complexity
Taking semantics and context into account, data is converted into valuable information and is made available to higher-level systems. The focus is on configuration instead of programming.


Maximum Transparency
Access previously unused data, discover optimization potential and stay one step ahead of your competition.
